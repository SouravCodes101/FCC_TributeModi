Narendra Modi

The Prime Minister of India

Timeline of the premiership of Narendra Modi:

    May 2014

  • 26 May – Modi Sworn in as 14th Prime Minister of India at the Rashtrapati Bhawan, New Delhi.

    • October 2014

  • 2 October – PM launches Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan; initiates cleanliness drive at Valmiki Basti, New Delhi.

    • May 2015

  • 9 May – Modi launches Jan Suraksha Schemes.

    • November 2016

  • 8 Nov – Announcement of Demonetization of Indian currency denominations of Rs 500 and 1000.

    • May 2019

  • 23 May – Wins for the second time as Member of Parliament from the city of Varanasi.

    • December 2019

  • 11 December – Rajya Sabha passes the Citizenship Amendment Bill.

    • March 2020

  • 24 March – Prime Minister applied national wide lockdown on seeing amid covid pandemic.

    • September 2020

  • 27 September – The President of India, Ram Nath Kovind gave his assent on the approved three farm reform bills.
